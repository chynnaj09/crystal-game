CRYSTALS COLLECTOR

MAGIC NUMBER:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four skull crystals below. By clicking on a skull you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to random number, you lose the game if your total score gets above the random number. The value of each skull is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each skull.
